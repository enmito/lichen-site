Privacy Policy

Silo — Local Tab Manager  |  Last updated: April 16, 2026

Silo is a local-first extension. All data you save is stored exclusively on your own device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

1. Overview

Silo ("the Extension") is a Chrome browser extension developed by Lichen. Its sole purpose is to let you save and restore browser tab collections locally on your device. This privacy policy explains what data the Extension handles, how it is stored, and what third-party services (if any) are involved.

2. Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data.

Specifically, Silo does not collect, transmit, log, or share:

  • Your name, email address, or any identifying information
  • Browsing history or tab URLs beyond what is needed in the moment to save a collection
  • Usage statistics or analytics events
  • Crash reports or telemetry
  • Any data with Lichen or any third-party analytics service

3. Data Stored Locally

All collection data (tab URLs, titles, favicons, labels, and settings) is stored exclusively in your browser's local IndexedDB storage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not synced to any cloud service
  • Is not accessible to Lichen or any third party
  • Can be exported or deleted by you at any time from within the extension

4. Permissions Explained

Silo requests the following Chrome permissions. Each is used only for the stated purpose and nothing else:

  • tabs — Read the URL, title, and favicon of your currently open tabs so they can be saved to a collection. Tab data is written to local IndexedDB only.
  • storage — Save extension settings (e.g., theme preference, free/Pro status) using Chrome's local storage API.
  • tabGroups — (Pro feature) Read and restore Chrome tab group names and colors alongside your saved collections. Only used when you trigger a save or restore action.
  • downloads — Allow you to export your collections as a JSON file or trigger an automatic local backup file download. No data is sent to any server.
  • alarms — (Pro feature) Schedule a periodic background task that triggers an automatic local backup. The alarm fires locally; no network request is made.

5. Payment Processing (Pro License)

The Pro license is handled by ExtensionPay, which uses Stripe as its payment processor. When you purchase a Pro license:

  • Payment information (card details, billing address) is collected and processed solely by ExtensionPay / Stripe, in accordance with their respective privacy policies.
  • Lichen does not receive, store, or have access to your payment card details.
  • ExtensionPay may store a minimal record (e.g., your email address) to verify your license status. Please review the ExtensionPay Privacy Policy and the Stripe Privacy Policy for full details.
  • The extension makes a license verification request to ExtensionPay's servers solely to confirm Pro status. No tab data or collection content is included in this request.

6. No Third-Party Analytics or Tracking

Silo does not embed any analytics SDK, tracking pixel, or telemetry library. No usage events, error logs, or behavioral data are sent anywhere.

7. Children's Privacy

Silo is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. Because no personal data is collected by the extension at all, no special handling is required.
